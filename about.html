Cleo Henry is a 26 year old artist based in Cape Town, South Africa.
Her body of work is influenced mainly by her surroundings and her personal experiences throughout her life. Her primary mediums are pencil and fine liner.Capturing the face is a big part of her body of work and remains to be her most favourite art practice. Currently, she is a student at Vega, a school that focuses on brand leadership. She has previously studied at the Michaelis School of Fine Art and also possesses her diploma in make up artistry. What she is aiming for in her future is creating something that will empower free thinkers in South Africa.
